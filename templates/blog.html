My Tech Journey ✍️

Hi, I’m Afzal Khan—a B.Tech Computer Science student at Lovely Professional University, passionate about building impactful solutions and constantly exploring new technologies. This space is my digital corner, where I share my projects, experiences, and everything I’m learning along the way.

My journey with code began in my early academic years, driven by curiosity and a desire to solve real-world problems. Over time, that interest has grown into a deep passion—whether I’m crafting Android apps that promote social good, building insightful dashboards to visualize data, or designing interactive web experiences. While I enjoy working across the stack, I particularly thrive when blending backend logic with clean, functional frontends.

I’ve worked on diverse projects—from GiveBack, a donation tracking app for NGOs, to data-driven dashboards that highlight energy usage and road accident trends. Each project has taught me something valuable—not just about technology, but about people, problems, and the power of thoughtful design.

More than just coding, I love understanding the why behind a project. What’s the story? What impact does it have? This mindset helps me build with intention, keeping users and purpose at the core. I'm also a keen learner who believes in growing through feedback, collaboration, and the occasional mistake.

This site is where I document it all—from successes and lessons to things I’m building or experimenting with. It’s not just a portfolio—it’s a growing archive of my journey in tech.

So if you’re into tech, data, apps, or just curious ideas, I hope you’ll join me. The journey is far from over, and I’d love to share it with you.
